Road Maintenance SOP
Document ID: SOP-2024-RM-002
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. POTHOLE REPAIR GUIDELINES

1.1 Priority Classification
- Priority 1 (Critical): Major roads, high traffic, safety hazard
  Timeline: 48 hours
- Priority 2 (High): Arterial roads, moderate traffic
  Timeline: 7 business days  
- Priority 3 (Medium): Local streets, low traffic
  Timeline: 15 business days

1.2 Repair Standards
- Minimum patch size: 30cm x 30cm
- Depth: Match existing road level
- Materials: Cold mix (temporary), hot mix (permanent)
- Compaction: 95% standard Proctor density
- Curing time: 24 hours before opening to traffic

2. INSPECTION AND REPORTING

2.1 Regular Inspections
- Major roads: Weekly
- Arterial roads: Bi-weekly
- Local streets: Monthly

2.2 Citizen Complaints
- Online portal available 24/7
- Mobile app for photo uploads
- Helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX
- Response within 24 hours

3. MONSOON PREPAREDNESS

3.1 Pre-Monsoon Activities (May-June)
- Clear all drainage channels
- Fill existing potholes
- Stockpile repair materials
- Deploy emergency response teams

3.2 During Monsoon
- Daily inspections of critical roads
- 24-hour emergency team on standby
- Temporary repairs as needed
- Permanent repairs post-monsoon

4. CONTRACTOR MANAGEMENT

4.1 Quality Standards
- All contractors must be certified
- Regular quality audits
- Penalty clauses for delays
- Performance bonds mandatory

4.2 Payment Terms
- 70% on completion
- 20% after 30-day inspection
- 10% after 90-day defect liability period

5. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

Non-compliance with timelines may result in:
- Written warning (1st offense)
- Suspension of duties (2nd offense)
- Termination (3rd offense)

All repairs must be documented with before/after photos.

END OF DOCUMENT
